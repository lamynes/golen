383ci 440hp LT1 Long Block Engine

Base Price $6,799

The 383/440 LT1 is an excellent performing engine package designed
for the LT1 Camaro and Firebird that won’t break the bank.

Peak Horsepower: 440bhp @ 6300rpm 
Peak Torque: 460lb/ft @ 4000rpm
Compression Ratio: 11.5:1
Operating Range: 1800 – 6300rpm
Required Fuel: 91+ Octane Pump Gasoline
