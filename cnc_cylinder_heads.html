Golen LS CNC Cylinder Heads

The Golen LS CNC Cylinder Heads are a high-performance cylinder head option for LS engines. These cylinder heads are 5-axis CNC ported and come in 4 and 6-bolt versions. They feature thick .750" decks, which are great for boosting.

The machine work on these cylinder heads is impressive. They are fully 5-axis CNC ported and hand blended. They also have a 3-angle competition valve job and the decks are milled to the customer’s desired size for compression. The cylinder heads are final washed and assembled to match the customer’s camshaft.

The included components of the Golen LS CNC Cylinder Heads are Manley stainless steel 2.165" intake valves and 1.600" exhaust valves. They also come with PAC Racing valve springs with steel retainers (titanium retainer options available) and billet rocker arm pedestals.

These cylinder heads are a great option for those looking to upgrade their LS engine and improve its performance.
