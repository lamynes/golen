ROAD RACE

We offer custom road race engine builds for all classes SCCA and NASA. We specialize in the Chevy LS Series but also build quite a few Small Block Chevy and Ford race engines as well. We have the latest technology in machining equipment to properly blue print each engine for longevity and performance. Offering dry sump systems from Daily and ARE we can supply a system that fits your race car without a problem.

  • Custom engine design
  • Power levels up to 1000hp
  • Dry Sump Systems
  • Free estimates

DRAG RACE

We build custom drag race engines of all types and specialize in the Chevy LS Series engines both fuel injected and carbureted versions. We also have extensive knowledge in both small and big block Chevy drag race engines as well. Boosted versions of these engines are no problem up to 1500hp using ATI Pro Chargers or turbo applications. We have two brand new Superflow engine dynos and test and tune each engine before it is delivered to the customer.
