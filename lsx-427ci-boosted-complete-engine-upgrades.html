a 1st Gen Camaro

Our entire line of Chevy LS engines can be custom built to be a direct bolt in for any vehicle so weather you have a GM car that came from the factory with a LS based engine or you have a early hot rod that you would like to retro fit we have you covered. Check out the options below and keep in mind that we offer full engine custom builds so if you do not see what you are looking for just ask.

LSx 427ci Boosted Complete Engine Upgrades

Our 427ci LSx Boosted Engine can be set up to run either a turbo or super charger with a max of 1400hp. It has a super strong lower engine featuring a Callies Dragon Slayer crank and Ultra H-Beam rods and a Wiseco boost ready piston. We can custom build this engine to meet your power goals and we offer super charger kits from Whipple and ATI Procharger. If you need recommendations for fuel system and tuning we can help and offer Holley EFI kits that work great with this level of power.
